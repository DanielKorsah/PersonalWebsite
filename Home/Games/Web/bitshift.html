Bitshift Colour Palette Prototype

This is a Prototype for the purposes of getting feedback for further development of this small toy project.

Controls:

  • Change background colour - Hold 1 and cycle with up and down arrow keys.
  • Change live wire colour - Hold 2 and cycle with up and down arrow keys.
  • Change non-live wire colour - Hold 3 and cycle with up and down arrow keys.
  • Flip input bit between 1 and 0 - Click on the bit you want to change.
WebGL embed hosted on Simmer.io service.
